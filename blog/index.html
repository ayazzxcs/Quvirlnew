How DropTrend uses these methods

DropTrend combines three groups of signals: Google Trends movement, Amazon marketplace demand and supplier data from CJdropshipping. The goal is not to promise guaranteed sales. The goal is to help sellers build a shortlist faster and then make better human decisions about audience, creative angle, price and risk.

Each guide explains one part of that process so users can understand the reasoning behind the product database instead of only seeing product cards.
